# Content Strategy for Wealth Navigator

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: High Net Worth Individuals (HNWI) with annual income of 10M - 20M JPY.
- **Mental Model**: Seeking "Asset Defense", "Tax Optimization", "Opportunity Loss Avoidance".
- **Key Logic**: Emphasize opportunity loss. "Not acting now is a risk."

## Content Categories & Topics

### 1. Domestic Real Estate (Domestic)
*Focus: Stability, Tax Inheritance, Re-pricing*
1. **2戸目の壁（属性維持戦略）**: Breaking through the financing wall for the second property.
2. **23区賃上げ（債務希釈化）**: Inflation as a tool to dilute debt.
3. **令和8年度相続税改正（実需型区分への移動）**: Impact of tax reforms on property types.
4. **大阪中之島再開発（リプライシング予測）**: Investment potential in redevelopment areas.
5. **45歳のローンデッドライン**: Age limits for optimal financing (35-year loans).
6. **中古マンションの「管理」格差**: Management quality as asset value.
7. **都心ワンルームの出口戦略**: Exit strategies for compact units.
8. **金利上昇局面の固定vs変動**: Interest rate strategies in 2026.
9. **法人化の損益分岐点**: When to incorporate for real estate.
10. **タワマン節税の規制後戦略**: Strategies after recent tax rule changes.

8. **ドバイ不動産のゴールデンビザ**: Residency perquisites.
9. **海外送金の2026年問題**: Regulatory changes in cross-border transfers.
10. **プレビルドの「権利転売」市場**: Flipping strategies.

### 3. Asset Management & Trends (Trends)
*Focus: Macro Trends, Alternative Assets, Hooks*

## Writing Guidelines
- **Structure**: Use standard HTML tags (`h2`, `h3`, `p`, `ul`, `li`, `table`).
- **Tone**: Professional, authoritative, slightly urgent (Fear Of Missing Out - FOMO).
- **Format**:
    - Start with a strong hook (data/fact).
    - Use `<expert_tip>` boxes for professional insights.
    - Include a comparison table (`<table>`) in the middle.
    - End with a clear CTA to the inquiry form or Line.

## 成果物2: 動画生成AI(Veo/Sora)用プロンプト集

## AI Video Prompts
- Prompt 1: Dark, cinematic shot of a slick real estate seminar in Tokyo, high contrast, ominous atmosphere, focusing on the presenter's confident gestures and the uneasy expressions of the attendees.
- Prompt 2: A sinking ship labeled 'Variable Rate' in stormy seas, with gold coins spilling into the dark ocean. The ship is listing heavily, and the sky is dark and foreboding, creating a sense of impending doom.
- Prompt 3: Abstract concept of a golden contract gradually morphing into handcuffs or chains, emphasizing the transition from allure to entrapment. The background should be a dark, luxurious aesthetic, suggesting hidden dangers beneath the surface.
- Prompt 4: A wide, establishing shot of a well-maintained, low-rise vintage apartment building in central Tokyo (Chiyoda, Chuo, or Minato ward). The scene should convey a sense of stability, enduring value, and urban sophistication, with emphasis on the surrounding cityscape and upscale neighborhood.
- Prompt 5: A split-screen showing two contrasting scenarios: on one side, a person drowning in debt paperwork, overwhelmed and stressed; on the other side, a calm, confident individual reviewing financial reports in a stylish, minimalist office, highlighting the differences between reckless leverage and prudent investment.

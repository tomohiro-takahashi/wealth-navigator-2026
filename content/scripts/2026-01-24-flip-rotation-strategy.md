# 動画台本: The Future of Real Estate Investing: Flipping Your Way to Profits

## Scene 1 (The Protagonist)
**ナレーション:**
Meet Takeshi, a real estate investor earning over 15 million yen per year. He thought he had it all figured out, but his dream of building wealth through real estate just turned into a nightmare.

**画面テキスト:**
Takeshi, High-Income Investor

## Scene 2 (The Conflict)
**ナレーション:**
Rising interest rates have turned Takeshi's newly built luxury apartment into a money pit. With negative cash flow and properties he can't sell, he's watching his assets melt away.

**画面テキスト:**
Bleeding Cash, Unsellable Properties

## Scene 3 (The Turnaround)
**ナレーション:**
Takeshi realized his mistake - he blindly trusted the bank's advice and developers, instead of managing his assets actively. The key to weathering this storm is to stop being a passive investor and become a savvy flipper. By renovating and quickly reselling undervalued properties, he can avoid the interest rate risk and inflation ravaging the market.

**画面テキスト:**
The Flipper's Advantage: Avoid Interest Rate Risk

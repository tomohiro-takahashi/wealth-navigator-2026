## AI Video Prompts

- Prompt 1: A high-resolution drone shot of the Metro Manila Subway construction site, showcasing the large-scale infrastructure project, bustling with construction workers and heavy machinery, under a clear blue sky. The scene should evoke a sense of progress and anticipation.
- Prompt 2: An aerial view of the Manila Metro Rail Transit System route, highlighting the key stations like Quezon Avenue Station, Ortigas Station, Bonifacio Global City Station, and Ninoy Aquino International Airport Terminal 3 Station. The scene should depict the urban landscape with traffic flow, emphasizing the potential for improved commutes.
- Prompt 3: A time-lapse video of the Bonifacio Global City skyline, showing the modern buildings and vibrant city life. The scene should transition from day to night, capturing the energy and dynamism of the business district. The video should convey the idea of growth and investment potential.
- Prompt 4: A realistic depiction of a typical RFO (Ready For Occupancy) condo unit interior in Manila, showcasing its modern design, comfortable living space, and stunning city views. The scene should emphasize the convenience and desirability of RFO properties near the subway stations.
- Prompt 5: Visualize a graph showcasing the land price appreciation along the Manila Metro Subway line, illustrating the shift from speculative "expectation" to tangible "real demand" following the completion of land acquisition. The animation should clearly indicate a significant upward trend after March 2026.

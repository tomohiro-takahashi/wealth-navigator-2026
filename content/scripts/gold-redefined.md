## AI Video Prompts

- Prompt 1: A cinematic shot of gold bars being stacked high in a massive vault, illuminated by soft, golden light. Focus on the intricate details of the gold, highlighting its texture and shine. In the background, subtle hints of advanced security systems.
- Prompt 2: A hyperrealistic animation showing the value of the US dollar plummeting against the rising value of gold. The animation starts with the Nixon Shock in 1971 and accelerates to present day. The chart is visually dramatic.
- Prompt 3: A global map highlighting countries like China, India, and Saudi Arabia as they gradually increase their gold reserves. Use vibrant colors and animated data points to show the shift in financial power away from the US dollar and towards gold.
- Prompt 4: A close-up shot of a person's hand carefully placing a gold coin into a secure vault. The lighting is dim and focused on the coin, emphasizing its value and importance as a form of protection against financial uncertainty. The vault is filled with other gold coins and bars.
- Prompt 5: Create a cinematic scene depicting a financial reset. Paper money is dissolving into dust, while gold coins and bars retain their luster and value. The overall tone is dramatic and cautionary.

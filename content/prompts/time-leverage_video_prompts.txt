# Video Construction Prompts for Generative AI
Project: 不動産投資の本質：年収3000万の奴隷から脱出する『時間のレバレッジ』
Description: Use these prompts in tools like Sora, Runway Gen-2, or Pika to generate short clips for each scene.

## Scene 1 (HOOK) - 7.5s
**Prompt**: Cinematic shot, a successful man in a tailor-made suit looking out of a luxury high-rise window at night, reflection in glass, somber but thoughtful expression. No text, no UI, clean footage.
**Context**: あなたは年収いくらの
「奴隷」ですか？

## Scene 2 (TRUTH_1) - 7.5s
**Prompt**: Fast-paced time-lapse of a busy city intersection at night, blurred lights of moving cars, transition to a luxury watch on a wrist. No text, no subtitles, cine-style.
**Context**: 労働収入 ＝
時間の切り売り

## Scene 3 (TRUTH_2) - 7.5s
**Prompt**: Close-up of a fountain pen signing a high-quality document, slow motion, elegant office setting. No text, no UI, professional lighting.
**Context**: 「不労所得」ではない
「時間のレバレッジ」だ

## Scene 4 (SOLUTION_1) - 7.5s
**Prompt**: A modern, high-end apartment interior, morning sun streaming through windows, dust dancing in light, peaceful atmosphere. No text, clean footage.
**Context**: あなたの代わりに
24時間働く「資産」
